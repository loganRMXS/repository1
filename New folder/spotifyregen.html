Questions


We got answers

Find more answers in our support site.
  • How does the SoundMix Pro trial work?
    If you've never had Pro before, you may be eligible for a free (or reduced rate) trial.

    For trials, you'll still need to enter a valid payment method to sign up. We'll use this to confirm your country or region and take payments if you want to
    keep Pro after the offer ends.

    We'll send you a reminder 7 days before your trial ends. Country restrictions and Terms apply.
  • How do I cancel my Pro plan?
    You can easily cancel your Pro plan any time online on your account page. Your Pro stays until your next billing date, then your account switches
    over to our free plan.

    You'll keep all your playlists, saved music, and be able to listen with ads when your account is free.
  • How does the Pro Duo plan work?
    Pro Duo is a plan for a couple who lives together. Compared with having two full-price Pro Individual accounts, it's also cheaper. We'll ask for
    your address to ensure you live at the same address. You can invite a member to the plan right after the purchase. Each member has their own separate
    Pro account and can listen at the same time with their own account. All saved music and playlists are their own. The other account holder cannot see
    what you are listening to.

    Read more info about Pro Duo.
  • How does the Pro Family plan work?
    Pro Family is a plan for up to 6 family members who live together. Compared with everyone having their own full-price Pro Individual account,
    it's also cheaper. We will ask for your address to ensure you live at the same address. You can invite members to the plan right after the purchase. Each
    member has their own separate Premium account and can listen at the same time with their own account. All saved music and playlists are their own.
    Other account holders cannot see what you are listening to.

    Read more info about Pro Family.
  • How does the Pro Student Plan work?
    If you are a student enrolled at an accredited college or university, and above the age of 16, then yes. You can get Pro Student for up to 4 years.

    Read more info about Premium Student.
  • How much is SoundMix Pro in Nigeria?
    The SoundMix Pro prices in Nigeria are different depending on which Premium plan you choose: The SoundMix Pro Individual plan costs NGN 8,300
    per month, the Pro Duo plan costs NGN 13,700 per month, the Pro Family plan costs NGN 20,000 per month, the Pro Student plan costs
    NGN 6500 per month. If you've never had a Pro plan before, you may be eligible for a free (or reduced rate) trial. Country restrictions and terms apply.
